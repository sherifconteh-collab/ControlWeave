# Audit Policy Center

Use this section for audit-ready policy documentation and testing workflows.

This is the policy-and-audit counterpart to the Developer Resources section.

## Purpose

The Audit Policy Center standardizes how ControlWeave documents:

- Policy intent and mandatory requirements
- Control mappings to frameworks
- Evidence expectations and retention
- Audit test procedures and outcomes
- Exception handling and approvals

## Core Sections

### 1. Policy Library

Create or maintain policy pages that define enforceable requirements.

- Information Security Policy (planned)
- Access Control Policy (planned)
- Data Retention and Disposal Policy (planned)
- AI Governance Policy (planned)
- [Vulnerability Management](security/Vulnerability-Management)

### 2. Audit Planning

Define what will be tested and when.

- In-scope systems and controls
- Audit period and cadence
- Roles and responsibilities
- Sampling method
- Pass/fail criteria

### 3. Control Testing

Execute control tests with consistent evidence handling.

- Test objective
- Procedure steps
- Sample details
- Result and severity
- Corrective action requirements

### 4. Evidence Management

Maintain traceable records for each control and test.

- Required evidence artifacts
- Ownership and storage location
- Retention period
- Chain-of-custody notes

### 5. Findings and Remediation

Track issues from identification to closure.

- Finding description
- Risk and impact
- Root cause
- Corrective action plan
- Verification and closure date

## Required Templates

Use these templates for consistency:

- [Policy Page Template](Policy-Page-Template)
- [Audit Test Plan Template](Audit-Test-Plan-Template)

## Governance Requirements

Every policy page must include:

- Owner
- Approver
- Effective date
- Review cadence
- Version history
- Exception handling process

Every audit plan page must include:

- Control scope
- Test method
- Evidence references
- Reviewer sign-off
- Final disposition

## Suggested Review Cadence

- Policy review: quarterly minimum
- Audit test plan review: every audit cycle
- Evidence retention check: monthly
- Open findings review: weekly until closure
