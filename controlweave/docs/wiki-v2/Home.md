## 🎓 Learning Paths

Choose your path based on your role and tier:

### 👤 For Community Tier Users
**Goal**: Master core GRC features

1. [Quick Start](Getting-Started) - Get familiar with the platform
2. [Framework Selection](Frameworks) - Choose your compliance frameworks (up to 10)
3. [Control Implementation](Controls) - Implement security controls
4. [Basic Assessments](Assessments) - Run your first assessment
5. [Evidence Management](Evidence) - Upload and organize evidence
6. [AI Copilot Basics](AI-Copilot) - Use your 10 monthly AI requests wisely

**Ready for more?** [Upgrade to Starter for CMDB, auditor tools, and 5x more AI →](https://controlweave.com/pricing)

### 🚀 For Pro Tier Users
**Goal**: Leverage asset management and auditor features

1. Complete Community tier path above
2. [CMDB Setup](CMDB-Overview) - Track your assets (up to 50)
3. [Vulnerability Management](Vulnerability-Management) - Track and remediate vulnerabilities
4. [Auditor Workspace](Auditor-Workspace) - Set up external auditor access
5. [SBOM/AIBOM](SBOM) - Generate software bills of materials
6. [Advanced AI Features](AI-Copilot) - Use your 50 monthly AI requests

**Need more?** [Upgrade to Professional for unlimited everything →](https://controlweave.com/pricing)

### ⭐ For Pro Tier Users
**Goal**: Advanced automation and unlimited scaling

1. Complete Pro tier features
2. [Advanced AI Analysis](Compliance-Forecast) - Unlimited AI-powered insights
3. [Custom Dashboards](Dashboard) - Build personalized compliance views
4. [Advanced Reporting](Reports) - Generate executive reports
5. [Multiple Frameworks](Frameworks) - Manage unlimited frameworks
6. [Team Scaling](User-Management) - Add unlimited users

**Enterprise needs?** [Contact sales for SSO, SIEM, and dedicated support →](https://controlweave.com/contact)

### 🏢 For Enterprise Tier Users
**Goal**: Enterprise integration and dedicated support

1. Complete Pro tier features
2. [SSO/SAML Setup](SSO-Setup) - Enable single sign-on
3. [SIEM Integration](SIEM-Integration) - Connect your SIEM
4. [API Integration](API-Documentation) - Build custom integrations
5. [Dedicated Support](Dedicated-Support) - Work with your CSM
6. [Advanced Security](Enterprise-Security) - Enterprise-grade controls
