# 📰 Regulatory News Guide

Stay ahead of compliance changes with an AI-powered regulatory intelligence feed tailored to your active frameworks.

## Overview

The Regulatory News module delivers a curated feed of regulatory updates, policy changes, and compliance news relevant to your organization's active frameworks. News items are automatically fetched and matched to your compliance profile.

---

## What You'll See

The regulatory news feed shows:

- **Title** — the regulatory update or news headline
- **Source** — which regulatory body or publication issued the update
- **Impact Level** — how significantly this update may affect your compliance posture
- **Relevant Frameworks** — which of your active frameworks are affected
- **Publication Date** — when the update was published
- **Read/Unread status** — track what you've already reviewed

---

## Navigating the Feed

### Step 1: Open Regulatory News

1. Click **Regulatory News** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see the news feed with unread items highlighted

### Step 2: Filter News Items

Use the filter options to narrow the feed:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Read Status** | Unread, Read, All |
| **Archived** | Active, Archived, All |
| **Framework** | Filter by specific framework (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA) |
| **Impact Level** | Low, Medium, High, Critical |
| **Source** | Specific regulatory body or publication |

### Step 3: Read an Item

1. Click any news item to open it
2. The full details are displayed
3. The item is automatically marked as **read**

---

## Managing News Items

### Mark as Read

- **Individual item**: Click to open it (automatically marked read)
- **All at once**: Click **Mark All as Read** to clear your unread count

### Archive Items

Archive news items you've reviewed and no longer need in your active feed:

1. Open a news item
2. Click **Archive**
3. The item moves to your archived view

To view archived items, set the **Archived** filter to "Archived."

### Refresh the Feed

To fetch the latest regulatory updates:

1. Click **Refresh News** (or the refresh icon)
2. New items are fetched and added to your feed
3. A confirmation shows how many new items were added
4. The refresh action is logged in the audit trail

---

## Unread Count

The unread count badge appears on the **Regulatory News** sidebar item showing how many unread items you have. This count is also accessible via the API for notification integrations.

---

## How News is Sourced

Regulatory news is gathered from authoritative sources relevant to your active frameworks:

- Official regulatory body publications (e.g., NIST, HHS, FTC, CISA)
- Legislative updates for AI laws and privacy regulations
- Industry standard body announcements (ISO, IEEE)
- Government cybersecurity advisories

Items are automatically matched to your **active frameworks** so you only see updates relevant to what your organization is tracking.

> **💡 Tip**: Activate more frameworks in your **Frameworks** settings to receive a broader set of regulatory intelligence relevant to your industry.

---

ControlWeaver has no tier gating — Regulatory News, news filtering, manual refresh, and archive management are all available to every authenticated user.
