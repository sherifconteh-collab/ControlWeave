# ControlWeave Coworker Sales Enablement Overview

## Goal
Enable any teammate to run a consistent outbound motion on LinkedIn + email, deliver the right demo account tier, and convert to a paid plan.

## Core Positioning (Use in every channel)
- ControlWeave is an AI-powered GRC platform for multi-framework compliance.
- Key value: crosswalk intelligence, evidence automation, and audit-ready workflows.
- Buyer outcome: less manual compliance work, clearer risk posture, faster readiness for audits.

## ICP Segments and Message Angles
- **Security & Compliance Leads (SMB/Mid-market):** simplify fragmented framework work and evidence collection.
- **CISO / GRC Program Owners (Enterprise):** reduce audit friction and centralize controls across teams.
- **Regulated Utilities / Critical Infrastructure:** map operational controls to utility-relevant frameworks and accelerate reporting.

## Tier-to-Use-Case Demo Mapping
- **Community (`admin@community.com`)**: early-stage teams evaluating core control/evidence workflows.
- **Pro (`admin@pro.com`)**: scaling programs needing stronger automation and broader framework coverage.
- **Enterprise (`admin@enterprise.com`)**: advanced, multi-team GRC operations with deeper audit workflows.
- **GovCloud (`admin@govcloud.com`)**: public sector, defense, and critical infrastructure evaluations with stricter environment requirements.

Default demo password: `ControlWeave!2026`

## Standard Outbound Workflow (Coworker Feature)
1. Pick one ICP and one pain point.
2. Send a short LinkedIn touchpoint (problem + specific outcome).
3. If positive response, send a tailored demo account email within 30 minutes.
4. Ask for one concrete success criterion (e.g., “map 2 frameworks to one control set”).
5. Follow up in 24 hours with one insight and one CTA.
6. Move to a 20-minute guided walkthrough tied to their buying timeline.

## Recommended Success Criteria by Stage
- **Initial response:** prospect confirms current compliance bottleneck.
- **Demo acceptance:** prospect logs in and reviews at least one workflow.
- **Evaluation fit:** prospect identifies a target tier and pilot scope.
- **Sales progression:** meeting booked with technical + business stakeholder.

## CRM / Activity Logging Checklist
- Segment, role, and primary pain point
- Which demo tier was shared
- Date/time of first response and follow-up
- Objections raised (price, integration, effort)
- Next step owner and due date
