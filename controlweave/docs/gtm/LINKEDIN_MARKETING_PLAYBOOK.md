# LinkedIn Marketing Playbook (ControlWeave)

## Objective
Generate qualified conversations with compliance/security decision-makers and convert interest into demo-account trials.

## Weekly Cadence
- **Mon:** POV post (compliance pain + strategic insight)
- **Tue:** Customer-like scenario post (before/after workflow)
- **Wed:** Product walkthrough clip/screenshots (feature + business impact)
- **Thu:** Framework/crosswalk education post
- **Fri:** CTA post (offer guided demo account by tier)

## Post Structure Template
1. Hook with a specific compliance pain
2. Name the operational impact
3. Show a short “what changed” with ControlWeave
4. Close with one CTA (comment or DM for tiered demo access)

## Sample Hooks
- "Most teams don’t fail audits because they lack controls—they fail because evidence is scattered."
- "If one control update requires touching 4 frameworks manually, your GRC process is leaking time."
- "Compliance maturity is not about volume of docs; it’s about traceability and repeatability."

## Comment-to-DM Flow
- Reply to comments within 2 hours
- Move interested users to DM with a single question:
  - "Which frameworks are highest priority for your team right now?"
- Route to matching demo tier and send credentials via email

## Content Guardrails
- Keep claims outcome-focused; avoid overpromising
- Use one clear CTA per post
- Tie features to business outcomes (time saved, audit readiness, clarity)
- Reuse customer language from discovery calls

## KPI Targets
- Profile views from ICP roles
- Connection acceptance rate
- Comment/DM conversion rate
- Demo-account acceptance rate
- Meetings booked from LinkedIn source

## Quick CTA Options
- "Comment ‘demo’ and I’ll send a tier-matched walkthrough account."
- "DM me your framework mix and I’ll map the best starting tier."
- "If you want a 20-minute practical walkthrough, message ‘pilot’."
