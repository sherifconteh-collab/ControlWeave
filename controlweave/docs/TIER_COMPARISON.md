ControlWeave offers four tiers to match your organization's needs:
- **Community** - Get started with core GRC features (free forever)
- **Pro** - Small teams and growing programs
- **Enterprise** - Comprehensive GRC with advanced features and premium support
- **Gov Cloud & Advisory** - Regulated-environment deployment plus hands-on advisory support

## Which Tier is Right for You?

### Choose **Community** if you:
- Are learning about GRC
- Have a very small team (5 users or fewer)
- Need only 1-2 frameworks
- Want full AI-powered analysis with your own API keys (BYOK)
- Want to try before committing
- Don't need CMDB or advanced features

### Choose **Pro** if you:
- Have a small team (up to 25 users)
- Need unlimited frameworks
- Want CMDB with up to 50 assets
- Need external auditor access
- Need vulnerability management
- Need Splunk or connector-based auto-evidence collection

### Choose **Enterprise** if you:
- Have a medium to large team or are an MSP
- Need unlimited frameworks, AI, and CMDB
- Want custom dashboards and reports
- Want dedicated support and SLAs
- Need integrations (Jira, Slack, custom)
- Want white-label reports
- Require dedicated infrastructure

### Choose **Gov Cloud & Advisory** if you:
- Financial services firm (RIA, broker-dealer, bank, TAMP)
- Need FINRA, SEC, or SR 11-7 compliance frameworks
- Deploy AI in advisory or risk workflows
- Require model risk management controls
- Need financial-services-specific regulatory monitoring

## Common Questions

### Can I try features before upgrading?
**Free Trial:** Enterprise and Gov Cloud & Advisory tiers offer 14-day trials. Contact sales.

### What happens if I hit my limit?
**Frameworks:** Can't activate more until upgrade
**AI Requests:** Blocked until next month or upgrade
**Users:** Can't invite more users

### Can I downgrade?
**Yes.** To downgrade to Community, go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Cancel Account**. This immediately cancels your Stripe subscription (if any) and downgrades to the Community tier. Your data is retained for 30 days.

To downgrade between paid tiers (e.g., Enterprise → Pro), go to **Settings** → **Available Plans** → **Switch to [Plan]**. Unused time is credited.

### Do limits reset?
**Monthly:** AI request limits reset monthly
**Annual:** Storage limits are annual totals

### Can I mix tiers across orgs?
**MSPs/Partners:** Yes, different organizations can have different tiers
