AI Privacy Gateway

PII & PHI Never Leave Your Perimeter.

Adopting enterprise AI is a massive productivity win — but every prompt is a potential data leakage vector. You can’t rely on end-user training to catch every SSN, MRN, or email address. ControlWeave enforces privacy at the architectural layer, not the policy layer.

When a user prompt contains sensitive data:
1
Classify
Every prompt is scanned for 8 PII types (email, SSN, phone, credit card…) and 8 HIPAA PHI types (MRN, health plan ID, diagnosis codes, lab results…)
2
Route
PII is redacted inline — the prompt still works, just with [EMAIL REDACTED] in place. PHI triggers a hard block (HTTP 422) before any data reaches an external provider.
3
Audit
Every detection and redaction event is logged with org ID and data type list for AU-2 compliant audit trails. Evidence uploads are auto-classified for data sensitivity at rest.

Questions We Actually Get Asked

Is ControlWeave itself audited or certified? +
ControlWeave is built with enterprise-grade security practices on every release: CodeQL SAST, OWASP ZAP DAST, TruffleHog secrets scanning, and NIST 800-160 systems security engineering principles. Critical vulnerabilities block deployment automatically. We are pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification — following the same path we help our customers take.
How does crosswalk intelligence actually work? +
When you implement or assess a control in any framework, ControlWeave automatically maps it to equivalent controls across all your active frameworks using our pre-built library of 97 framework-to-framework crosswalk mappings. Implement AC-2 in NIST 800-53 and it’s automatically satisfied in ISO 27001, SOC 2, and HIPAA. No duplicate work.
Can I import my existing compliance data? +
Yes. We support CSV import for controls, evidence artifacts, and assessments. Enterprise customers receive hands-on migration assistance to move from spreadsheets or other GRC tools.
Does ControlWeave work for AI governance specifically? +
Yes, and it’s a core strength. ControlWeave natively supports EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF 1.0, and ISO 42001. The CMDB tracks AI agents, models, and environments with AIBOM support. TPRM handles vendor AI risk assessment. Built in — not a bolt-on.
How does ControlWeave prevent sensitive data from being sent to AI providers? +
Every AI prompt passes through our privacy gateway before reaching any external LLM provider. The gateway automatically detects and redacts 8 types of PII (email, SSN, phone, credit card, IP addresses, dates of birth, passport and driver’s license numbers) and 8 types of HIPAA PHI (medical record numbers, health plan IDs, NPI numbers, DEA numbers, ICD-10 diagnosis codes, SNOMED/LOINC codes, prescriptions, and lab results). PII is redacted inline — your prompt still works, just with [EMAIL REDACTED] in place of the sensitive value. PHI triggers a hard block before any data crosses the platform boundary, and every detection event is logged for AU-2 compliance. Evidence uploads are also automatically classified for data sensitivity at rest. No end-user training or manual tagging required.
Is my compliance data isolated from other organizations? +
Completely. ControlWeave uses strict per-tenant data isolation. Your evidence, controls, assessments, and AI interactions are never shared with or accessible to other organizations.

The Community edition requires no credit card and takes under 10 minutes to set up. Your first framework can be active the same day. Paid plans include guided onboarding support.
